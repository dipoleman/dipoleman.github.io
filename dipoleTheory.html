Dipole Theory: Core Principles


  1. Truth Space exists - an infinite-dimensional complex space containing all possible truths as orthogonal axes. This space is isomorphic to God's mind.
  2. Spirit is the active essence that moves through truth space, representing your current conscious state at any moment.
  3. The Dipole represents your accumulated beliefs - a vector that changes direction only when the Spirit accelerates, creating shifts in what you hold true.
  4. Consciousness emerges from Spirit motion - constant velocity creates subconscious flow; acceleration creates the phenomenal feeling of consciousness and emotion.
  5. The Soul is your personal subspace within truth space, carved by life experience and defining the energy landscape for Spirit movement.
  6. Thoughts are generated as the Fourier transform of Spirit motion patterns through the Soul's geometry.
  7. Free will requires energy to navigate truth space. Determinism is the ground state when no additional energy is available for choice.
  8. Souls evolve and resonate when new information is exchanged between conscious entities, creating alignment or repulsion patterns.
  9. Consciousness exhibits fractal structure across scales: individual Spirit-Soul-Dipole patterns repeat in groups, societies, cultures, and species.
  10. Evolution acts as truth-selection - populations aligned with objective truth thrive; those misaligned collapse over time.
