DipoleTheory

In this text, I will present a theory of consciousness that connects the physical, mathematical and spiritual realms. I will argue that there is a space of all possible thoughts that transcends physical reality and contains God, the ultimate thought. I will also explain how conscious entities, such as humans, can move through this space with free will and form their souls and spirits. This theory is called dipole theory because it posits two poles of existence: the physical and the spiritual.

Dipole Theory: Assumptions


  1. There exists a space of all possible thoughts. The completion of this space is Gods Mind.
  2. The collection of all thoughts of a single entity is its Soul.
  3. The Soul has a magnitude and direction represented by the Soul Vector.
  4. The substance that moves through the space of all possible thoughts, contained within the Soul, is the Spirit.
  5. The Spirit has a magnitude and direction represented by the Dipole.
  6. Consciousness is the movement of the Dipole in the space of all possible thoughts.
  7. Movement of the spirit is constrained to a subspace of Gods mind, free will is the possible paths taken by the spirit in this subspace.
  8. When information is exchanged between conscious entities, the collection of thoughts change. Therefore the Souls evolve.
  9. Consciousness has a self-similar pattern at different scales, its fractal in nature.
  10. We can divide what exists into three realms:
    1. Physical universe, obeys the laws of physics, super-determinism.
    2. Platonic realm of mathematics, obeys the rules of logic.
    3. Space of all possible thoughts, allows free will.

Sphere Of All Possible Thoughts


(3D representation of the Space Of All Possible Thoughts)


First, select 1 Soul. Each point represents a single thought. The collection of all thoughts is represented by the Soul, and its direction is indicated by the Soul Vector. The moral plane is perpendicular to the Soul Vector.

Selecting + Soul will keep adding Souls. Press Clear to clear.

Selecting Axis shows the universal direction of Good and Evil.

When 2 Souls are selected, the dot product of their Soul Vectors is a measure of the similarity between the two Souls.

The breadth of knowledge is indicated with the Open, Normal, and Closed options.
